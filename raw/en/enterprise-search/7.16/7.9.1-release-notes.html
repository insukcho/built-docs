Upgrading to Enterprise Search 7.9.1? See Upgrading & migrating.

App Searchedit

Bug fixesedit

Bug fixesedit

  • Fixes large thumbnail missing from Confluence Server results.
  • Fixes minor display issues in the web search experience, including logos, icons, and buttons.
