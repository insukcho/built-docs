Subscribing from a marketplaceedit

Subscribe to Elasticsearch Service from a marketplace. Then, your subscription is available and billed together with other services that you’re already using. You can subcribe to Elasticsearch Service from any of the following:

  • AWS Marketplace
  • Azure Marketplace
  • Native Azure integration
  • GCP Marketplace
