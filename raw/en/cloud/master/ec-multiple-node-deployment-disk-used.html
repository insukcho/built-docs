Full disk on multiple-nodes deploymentedit

Health check

  • Inspect disk usage on all nodes.

Possible cause

  • The available storage is insufficient for the amount of ingested data.

Resolution

  • Delete data.
  • Increase disk size.
  • Enable autoscaling.
  • Configure index lifecycle management (ILM) policies.
  • Manage data tiers.
