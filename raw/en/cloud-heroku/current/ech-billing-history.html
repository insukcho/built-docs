Check your billing historyedit

Information about outstanding payments and billing receipts is available from the Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku console.

To check your billing history:

  1. Log in to the Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku console.
  2. Select the user icon on the header bar and choose Billing from the menu.
  3. On the History page, select the invoice number for a detailed PDF.
